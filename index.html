HOW IT WORKS

WHAT MAKES US THE BEST

We are dedicated to helping you achieve YOUR specific fitness goals. Our classes are designed to burn calories and shed fat fast! We’re known for producing results in an energetic, fun, and exciting environment. You’ll get in the best shape of your life, enjoying what you’re doing!

HOW WE CAN HELP YOU

Over the years we have formulated the perfect recipe for fitness-success. The convergence of our crafted kickboxing circuits to bring you superb conditioning, paired with the resistance training we incorporate into a single workout regimen is incredibly popular for one reason: it WORKS! Talk to our current members and they’ll use words like “Dynamic!” “Intense” “Muscle-burning” and “Body-sculpting.”

NO RISK – NO OBLIGATION

A single kickboxing class can burn twice as many calories as traditional exercise, and it’s more fun, too! You’ll see improvements in your balance, flexibility, strength, coordination, and stamina. Your muscles will become more supple and stronger. Best of all, you’ll look and feel great!

WHAT IS BURN?

Burn Kickboxing classes are fun, exciting, and effective. But it’s so much more than just a kickboxing class — it’s a powerful full body workout experience that is unlike anything else out there. We workout together as a team in a custom designed studio with high energy music and unique burn-mode elements that will have you feeling full of life. We’re led by energizing instructors, who support, coach, and push us to be our bests. We sweat, tone, smile, and of course… love to feel the BURN!
